CSC301H Demo Fest

CSC301H is an undergraduate course on "Introduction to Software Engineering" in the Department of Computer Science, UofT. To practice techniques learned in class, students work in teams to develop custom software for community groups and non-profit organizations.

Demo Fest is an opportunity for students in the course to show their projects. Each group will give a give a live demonstration and presentation for 10 minutes, followed by a 10 minutes of questions.

The general public is welcome to attend any of the sessions or for the duration of the day.
